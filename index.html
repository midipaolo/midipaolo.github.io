Why Study Tech?

Money

According to US News, the median salary for Software Developers in 2020 was $110,140. "The lowest-paid 25% made $84,020."

Creativity

The biggest creations that have changed the modern world have come from the world of tech. The solutions for the problems we face now will also likely be solved through modern technology.

Freedom

According to Terminal, 61% of developers work fully remote. Only 4% have jobs that are never done remotely.

Future

According to goremotely.net, "In 2017, there were less than 50,000 Computer Science graduates and over 500,000 open positions." and there are over 1 million software developer jobs currently available.

I'm 'Not a Coder'

Neither Are We..

You don't have to write code to work with technology.

HR

Every major company has a Human Resources department. If you need someone to interview and hire new employees, it would be helpful if they could at least understand the terminology and concepts in the tech world. Therefore, HR workers that have some tech experience are highly sought-after.

UI/UX

There has never been a better time in history to make money as an artist. Every app and every website needs someone to design the aesthetic elements. User interface designers understand design principles, but they have an awareness of how apps work, so that their designs fit the medium in which they're displayed.

Project Manager

Believe it or not, creating new features in apps is a very complect process, and it requires organized coordination of programmers, artists, sales, user experience designers, and others. In order to coordinate this work, companies often hire project managers to just make sure everything goes according to plan and remains on schedule. If you have a talent for organization and planning, you don't necessarily have to know how to code to work for a big tech company, earn a big salary, work remotely, and just organize the work of others.
